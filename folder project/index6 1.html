All About F1


The first ever F1 grand prixe was held at Silverstone in 1950.
In a season there is usually 20 active drivers. The driver with
the most point will win the WDC (world driver's championship).
The first race in a season is held in Australia and the last would
be held at UAE. There is 2 championships in F1 which are WDC
